§26 mā bhadanta kātyāyana sapracāram utpādabhaṅghayuktāṃ dharmatāṃ nirdiśa |yo bhadanta kātyāyanātyantatayā
na jāto na janiṣyati notpanno na niruddho na nirotsyate yam anityārthaḥ |yaḥ
pañcānāṃ skandhānāṃ śūnyatānugamānutpādanirodhārtho yaṃ duḥkhārthaḥ |yad ātmānātmayor advayatvam
ayaṃ anātmārthaḥ |yo svabhāvo parabhāvas tad anujjvalitam, yad anujjvallitaṃ na
tac chāmyati, yo atyantopaśamo yaṃ śāntārthaḥ |
