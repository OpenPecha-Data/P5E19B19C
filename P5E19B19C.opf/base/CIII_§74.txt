§74 sarvajñajñānānubodhanasarvakuśaladharmābhinirhṛtaḥ sarvabodhipakṣyadharmasamudgamaḥ

ayaṃ sa kulaputra dharmayajñaḥ,yatra dharmayajñe pratiṣṭhitā bodhisatvā iṣṭayajñayājūkā dakṣiṇīyā
bhavanti sadevakasya lokasya |evaṃ hi bhagavan tasya gṛhapater nirdiśatas tasyā brāhmaṇaparṣado
dvayor brāhmaṇaśatayor anuttarāyāṃ samyakṣaṃbodhau cittāny utpannāni |
