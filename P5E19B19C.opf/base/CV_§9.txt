§9 athāyuṣmāñ śāriputro vimalakīrtiṃ licchavim evam āha:āścaryaṃ kulaputra yad ihaivaṃ parītte
gṛha imānīyanti siṃhāsanasahasrāṇy evam uccāny evaṃ pragṛhītāni vicitrāṇi |na ca vaiśālyā
mahānagaryā āvaraṇaṃ kṛtam,na jambūdvīpasya, na grāmanagaranigamajanapadarāṣṭrarājadhānīnām,
na cāturmahādvīpikasya kiṃcid āvaraṇam, na devanāgayakṣagandharvāsuragaruḍakinnaramahoragāṇām
āvaraṇaṃ kṛtam |tathaiva saṃdṛśyante yathā pūrvaṃ tathā paścāt |
