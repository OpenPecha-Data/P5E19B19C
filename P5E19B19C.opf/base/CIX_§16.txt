§16 te bodhisatvā āhuḥ:āścaryaṃ bhagavataḥ śākyamuner yatra hi nāma pratisaṃhṛtya buddhamāhātmyaṃ
daridralūhatayā khaṭuṅkān satvān vinayati |ye ’pi bodhisatvā ihaivaṃ pratikaṣṭe buddhakṣetre
prativasanti teṣām apy acintyā mahākaruṇā |vimalakīrtir āha:evam eva satpuruṣāḥ tathaitad
yathā vadatha |ye ’pīha bodhisatvāḥ pratyājātāḥ, dṛḍhā teṣāṃ mahākaruṇā |te bahutaram
iha lokadhātāv ekajanmanā kariṣyanti satvārtham |na tv eva tatra sarvagandhasugandhe
lokadhātau kalpasahasreṇa satvārthaḥ |
