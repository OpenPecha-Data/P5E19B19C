§30 iyaṃ ca kathā pravṛttā vimalakīrtiś ca licchavis taṃ pradeśam upasaṃkrāmat |upasaṃkramya
mama pādau śirasā vanditvaivam āha:kiṃ bhadantāniruddha divyaṃ cakṣur abhisaṃskāralakṣaṇam
utānabhisaṃskāralakṣaṇam |yady abhisaṃskāralakṣaṇaṃ tad bāhyaiḥ pañcābhijñaiḥ samamathānabhisaṃskāralakṣaṇaṃ
anabhisaṃskāro ’saṃskṛtas tena na śakyaṃ draṣṭum |tat kathaṃ sthaviraḥ
paśyati |

so ’haṃ tūṣṇīm abhūvam |
