§18 tatra mañjuśrīḥ glānena bodhisatvenaivam ime dharmā nidhyapayitavyāḥ |yat kāyasya cittasya
ca vyādheś cānityaduḥkhaśūnyānātmapratyavekṣaṇā, iyam asya prajñā |yat punaḥ kāyavyādhipariharaṇatayā
na khidyate, na pratibadhnāti saṃsāram,satvārthayogam anuyuktaḥ, ayam asyopāyaḥ
|punar aparaṃ yat kāyasya vyādheś cittasya cāñonyaparāparatāṃ na nirnavatāniḥpurāṇatāṃ
pratyavekṣate, iyam asya prajñā |yat punaḥ kāyasya vyādheś cittasya ca nātyantopaśamaṃ nirodham
atyayati, ayam asyopāyaḥ |
