§13 atha tato bhojanāt sarvā sā parṣat tṛptā kṛtā |na ca tāvad bhojanaṃ kṣīyate |yaiś ca bodhisatvaiḥ
śrāvakaiḥ śakrabrahmalokapālais tadanyaiś ca satvais tad bhojanaṃ bhuktam,teṣāṃ
tādṛśaṃ sukhaṃ kāye ’vakrāntaṃ yādṛśaṃ sarvasukhapratimaṇḍite lokadhātau bodhisatvānāṃ sukham
|sarvaromakūpebhyaś ca teṣāṃ tādṛśo gandhaḥ pravāti |tadyathāpi nāma tasminn eva sarvagandhasugandhe
lokadhātau vṛkṣāṇāṃ gandhaḥ |
