§7 īdṛśasya bhadantamahāmaudgalyāyana dharmasya kīdṛśī deśanā |dharmadeśaneti bhadantamahāmaudgalyāyana
samāropapadam etat |ye pi śṛṇvanti te pi samāropeṇeva śṛṇvanti |yatra bhadanta
maudgalyāyana asamāropapadaṃ na tatra deśyate na śrūyate na vijñāyate |tadyathā māyāpuruṣo
māyāpuruṣebhyo dharmaṃ deśayati |
