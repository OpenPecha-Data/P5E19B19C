§21 atha khalu bhagavān āyuṣmantaṃ pūrṇaṃ maitrayāṇīputram āmatrayate smagaccha tvaṃ pūrṇa vimalakīrter
licchaver glānaparipṛcchakaḥ |

pūrṇo py āha nāhaṃ bhagavan utsahe tasya satpuruṣasya glānaparipṛcchako gantum |tat kasmād dhetoḥ
|abhijānāmy ahaṃ bhagavan ekasmin samaye vanasyānyatamasmin pṛthivīpradeśādikarmikāṇāṃ
bhikṣūṇāṃ dharmaṃ deśayāmi |

tatra me vimalakīrtir licchavir upasaṃkramya mām evam āha
