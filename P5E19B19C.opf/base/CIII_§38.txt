§38 tatra bhagavān āyuṣmantaṃ rāhulam āmantrayate sma:gaccha tvaṃ rāhula vimalakīrter licchaver
glānaparipṛcchakaḥ |

rāhulo ’py āha: nāhaṃ bhagavan utsahe tasya satpuruṣasya glānaparipṛcchako gantum |tat kasmād
dhetoḥ |abhijānāmy ahaṃ bhagavan: ekasmin samaye saṃbahulā licchavikumārakā yenāhaṃ tenopasaṃkramya
mām etad avocan:tvaṃ rāhula tasya bhagavataḥ putraś cakravartirājyam utsṛjya pravrajitaḥtatra
ke te pravrajyāyā guṇānuśaṃsāḥ |

teṣām ahaṃ yathārūpaṃ pravrajyāyā guṇānuśaṃsā nirdiśāmivimalakīrtir licchavir yenāhaṃ tenopasaṃkrāntaḥ
| sa mām abhivandyaitad avocat:
