§6 yaiś ca bhadantānanda bhikṣubhir anavakrāntaniyāmair etad bhojanaṃ bhuktaṃ teṣām avakrāntaniyāmānāṃ
pariṇaṃsyati |yaiḥ punar avakrāntaniyāmair bhuktaṃ teṣāṃ nāparimuktacittānāṃ pariṇaṃsyati
|yair anutpāditabodhicittaiḥ satvaiḥ paribhuktaṃ teṣām utpāditabodhicittānām pariṇaṃsyati
|yair utpāditabodhicittair bhuktaṃ teṣāṃ nāpratilabdhakṣāntikānāṃ pariṇaṃsyati
|yaiḥ punaḥ pratilabdhakṣāntikair bhuktaṃ teṣām ekajātipratibaddhānāṃ pariṇaṃsyati |
