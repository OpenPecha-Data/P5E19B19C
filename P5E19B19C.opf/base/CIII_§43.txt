§43 kiṃ bhadantānanda kālyam eva pātram ādāyāsmin gṛhadvārasamīpe tiṣṭhasi |

tam enam aham etad avocam: bhagavato gṛhapate kāyasya kaścid evābādhaḥ |tatra ca kṣīreṇa kṛtyam,
tat paryeṣāmi |

sa mām evam āha: alaṃ bhadantānanda mā evaṃ vocaḥ |vajrasaṃhatano hi bhadantānanda tathāgatakāyaḥ
sarvākuśalavāsanāprahīṇaḥ sarvamahaujaskakuśaladharmasamanvāgataḥ |kutas tasya vyādhiḥ
kuta upadravaḥ |
