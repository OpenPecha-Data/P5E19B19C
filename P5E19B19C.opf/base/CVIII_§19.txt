§19 sumatir bodhisatva āha:cakṣū rūpaṃ ca dvayam etat |yat punaś cakṣuḥparijñātāvī rūpeṣu na
rajyati na duṣyati na muhyati,sa ucyate śānta iti |śrotraṃ śabdāś ca ghrāṇaṃ gandhāś ca jihvā
rasāś ca kāyaḥ spraṣṭavyāni ca mano dharmāś ca dvayam etat |yat punar manaḥparijñātāvī
dharmeṣu na rajyate na duṣyati na muhyati,sa ucyate śānta iti |evaṃ śāntasthitasyādvayapraveśaḥ
