§3 na bhadantaśāriputra evaṃ pratisaṃlayanaṃ saṃlātavyaṃ yathā tvaṃ pratisaṃlīnaḥ |api tu tathā
pratisaṃlīyaś ca yathā traidhātuke na kāyaś cittaṃ vā saṃdṛśyate |tathā pratisaṃlīyaś
ca yathā nirodhāc ca na vyuttiṣṭhasi sarvaīryāpatheṣu ca saṃdṛśyase |tathā pratisamlīyaś ca
yathā prāptilakṣaṇaṃ ca na vijahāsi pṛthagjanalakṣaṇeṣu ca saṃdṛśyase |tathā pratisamlīyaś
ca yathā te na cādhyātmaṃ cittam avasthitaṃ bhaven na bahirdhopavicaret |tathā pratisamlīyaś
ca yathā sarvadṛṣṭigatebhaś ca na calasi saptatriṃśatsu ca bodhipakṣyeṣu dharmeṣu saṃdṛśyase
|tathā pratisaṃlīyaś ca yathā saṃsārāvacarāṃś ca kleśān na prajahāsi nirvāṇasamavasaraṇaś
ca bhavasi |ye bhadanta śāriputra evaṃ pratisaṃlayanaṃ pratisaṃlīyante teṣāṃ bhagavān
pratisaṃlayanam anujānāti |
