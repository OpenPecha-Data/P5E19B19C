§11 punar aparaṃ bhadanta śāriputro acintyavimokṣapratiṣṭhito bodhisatvo yaś caturṣu mahāsamudreṣv
apskandhas tam ekasmin romakūpe prakṣipet |na ca matsyakacchapaśiśumāramaṇḍūkānām anyeṣāṃ
vaudakānāṃ prāṇināṃ pīḍā bhavet |na ca nāgayakṣagandharvāsurāṇām evaṃ bhavet kasmin
vayaṃ prakṣiptāḥ |sā ca kriyā prajñāyeta | na ca kaścit satvo vihiṃsito viheṭhito vā bhavet
