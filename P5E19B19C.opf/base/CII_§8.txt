§8 teṣām upasaṃkrāntānāṃ vimalakīrtir licchaviḥ | imam eva cāturmahābhautikaṃ kāyam ārabhya
dharmaṃ deśayati sma:evam anityo ’yaṃ mārṣāḥ kāyaḥ, evam adhruvaḥ, evam anāsvāsikaḥ, evaṃ
durbalaḥevam asāraḥ, evaṃ jarjaraḥ, evam itvaraḥ, evaṃ duḥkhaḥ, evam ābādhikaḥ, evaṃ vipariṇāmadharmāevaṃ
bahurogabhājano ’yaṃ mārṣāḥ kāyaḥ | tatra paṇḍitena niśrayo na kartavyaḥ |
