§11 prādeśikī bhadanta mahākāśyapasya karuṇāmaitrī, yan mahākulāny utsṛjya daridrakulāny upasaṃkrāmasi
|api tu bhadanta mahākāśyapa dharma samatāpratiṣṭhithena te bhavitavyam | sarvadā
sarvasatvasamanvāhāreṇa piṇḍapātaḥ paryeṣṭavyaḥ |anāhāreṇa cāhāraḥ paryeṣṭavyaḥ |parapiṇḍa
grāhāpanayāya ca te piṇḍāya caritavyam |śunyagrāmādhiṣṭhithena ca grāmaḥ praveṣṭavyaḥ
|naranārīparipākāya ca te nagaraṃ praveṣṭavyam |buddha kulakulīnena ca te kulāny upasaṃkramitavyāni
