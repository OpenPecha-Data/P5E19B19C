§11 idam ānanda sarvabuddhadharmapraveśaṃ nāma dharmamukhaṃyatra yatra dharmamukhe praviṣṭo
bodhisatvaḥ sarvodāravyūhaguṇasamanvāgateṣu buddhakṣetreṣu nāvalīyate nāvanamati |sarvodāravyūhaguṇasamanvāgateṣu
ca buddhakṣetreṣu na kṛṣyati nonnamati |tathāgatānāṃ ca sakāśe so
’dhimātraṃ gauravam utpādayati |āścaryam idaṃ buddhānāṃ bhagavatāṃ sarvadharmasamatādhimuktānāṃ
satvaparipācanatayā buddhakṣetranānātvadarśanam |
