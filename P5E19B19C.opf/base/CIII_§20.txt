§20 iha nirdeśe diśyamāne dvayor devaputraśatayor virajo vigatamalaṃ dharmeṣu dharmacakṣur viśuddhampañcamānāṃ
ca devaputraśatānām ānulomikyāḥ kṣānteḥ pratilambho bhūt |ahaṃ ca niṣpratibhāno
bhūvam na cāsya śaknomy uttare prativacanaṃ dātumtan nāham utsahe tasya satpuruṣasya
glānaparipṛcchako gantum |
