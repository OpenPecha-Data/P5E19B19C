vyādhiḥ |yadā ca sarvasatvā vigatavyādhayo bhaviṣyanti tadā mama vyādhiḥ praśrabdho bhaviṣyati
|tat kasmād dhetoḥ |satvādhiṣṭhāno hi mañjuśrīḥ bodhisatvasya saṃsāraḥ | saṃsāraniśritaś
ca vyādhiḥ |yadā sarvasatvā vigatavyādhayo bhaviṣyanti tadā bodhisatvo ’rogo bhaviṣyati
