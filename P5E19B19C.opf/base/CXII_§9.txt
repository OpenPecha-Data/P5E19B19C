§9 tataś caiko rājakumāraḥ somacchatro nāma |tasyaikākino rahogatasyaitad abhūt:asti tv asyāḥ
pujāyā anyodāratā viśiṣṭatarā pūjeti |

tasyāntarīkṣād devatā buddhādhiṣṭhānenaivam āha:dharmapūjā satpuruṣa sarvapūjābhyo viśiṣyate
|

sa evam āha: katamā punaḥ sā dharmapūjeti |

devatāha: etam eva tvaṃ satpuruṣa tathāgataṃ bhaiṣajyarājam upasaṃkramya paripṛccha katamā sā
dharmapūjeti |sa te bhagavān vyākariṣyati |

atha devānām indra sa somacchatro rājakumāro yena bhagavān bhaiṣajyarājas tathāgato ’rhan samyakṣaṃbuddhas
tenopasaṃkrāmat |upasaṃkramya tasya bhagavataḥ pādau śirasā vanditvaikānte
’sthāt |ekāntasthitaś ca somacchatro rājakumāras taṃ bhagavantaṃ bhaiṣajyarājaṃ tathāgatam
etad avocat:dharmapūjā dharmapūjeti bhagavan ucyate | katamā sā dharmapūjeti |
