§5 athāyuṣmān ānando vimalakīrtiṃ bodhisatvam evam āha:kiyacciraṃ punar eṣa kulaputra gandho
’nuvartiṣyate |

āha: yāvad etad bhojanaṃ na pariṇataṃ bhaviṣyati |

ānanda āha: kiyaccireṇa punar etad bhojanaṃ pariṇaṃsyati |

āha: saptabhiḥ saptāhaiḥ pariṇaṃsyati |upari cāsya saptāham ojaḥ sphariṣyati |na cājīrṇadoso
bhaviṣyati |
