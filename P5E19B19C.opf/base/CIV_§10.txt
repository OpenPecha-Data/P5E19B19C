§10 api tu khalu punar mañjuśrīḥ kathaṃ bodhisatvena glānako bodhisatvaḥ pratisaṃmoditavyaḥ
|

mañjuśrīr āha: kāyānityatayā na ca nirvidvirāgatayā,kāyaduḥkhatayā na ca nirvāṇābhinandanatayā,kāyānātmatayā
satvaparipācanatayā ca,kāyaśāntatayā na cātyantaśamatayā,sarvaduścaritadeśanatayā
na ca saṃkrāntitaḥ,svaglānyena parasatvaglānakaruṇatayā,pūrvāntakoṭīduḥkhānusmaraṇatayā,satvārthakriyānusmaraṇatayā,kuśalamūlābhimukhīkaraṇatayā,ādiviśuddhatayā,aparitarṣaṇatayā,sadāvīryāraṃbhāc
ca,vaidyarājo bhaviṣyasi sarvavyādhīnāṃ śamayiteti |evaṃ bodhisatvena
bodhisatvo glānaḥ pratisaṃmoditavyaḥ |
