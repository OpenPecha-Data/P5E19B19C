sarvajñatā, sarvajñatāsvabhāva eva pariṇāmaḥ |evaṃ śīlaṃ kṣāntiṃ vīryaṃ dhyānaṃ prajñāṃ
sarvajñatāyāṃ pariṇāmayatīti dvayam etat |prajñāsvabhāvaiva sarvajñatā, sarvajñatāsvabhāva
