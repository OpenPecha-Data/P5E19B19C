§14 atha vimalakīrtir licchavir jānann eva tān bhagavato gandhottamakūṭasya tathāgatasya buddhakṣetrād
āgatān bodhisatvān etad avocat:kidṛśī kulaputrāḥ gandhottamakūṭasya tathāgatasya
dharmadeśanā |

ta evam āhuḥ:na sa tathāgato ’kṣaraniruktyā dharmaṃ deśayati |tenaiva gandhena te bodhisatvā
vinayaṃ gacchanti |yatra yatraiva gandhavṛkṣe te bodhisatvā niṣīdanti,tatas tatas tādṛśo
gandhaḥ pravātiyat samanantarāghrāte gandhe sarvabodhisatvaguṇākaraṃ nāma samādhiṃ pratilabhanteyasya
samādheḥ samanantarapratilambhāt sarveṣāṃ bodhisatvaguṇā jāyante |
