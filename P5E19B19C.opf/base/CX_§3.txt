§3 tatra bhagavān āyuṣmantaṃ śāriputram āmantrayate sma:dṛṣṭaṃ te śāriputra eṣām agrasatvānāṃ
bodhisatvānāṃ vikrīḍitam |

āha: dṛṣṭaṃ bhagavan |

bhagavān āha:tataḥ kā te saṃjñotpannā |

āha: acintyā saṃjñā me bhagavan tatrodapadyatayathā cintayituṃ tulayituṃ gaṇayituṃ na śaknomi
tādṛśīṃ teṣām acintyāṃ kriyāṃ paśyāmi |
