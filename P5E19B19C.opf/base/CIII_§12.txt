§12 apratigrahaṇatayā ca piṇḍapātaḥ pratigrāhyaḥ |jātyandhasamatayā ca rūpāṇi draṣṭavyāni |pratiśrutakopamatayā
ca śabdāḥ śrotavyāḥ |vātasamatayā ca gandhā ghrātavyāḥ |avijñaptito rasā
āsvādayitavyāḥ |jñānāsparśanatayā ca sparśāḥ spraṣṭavyāḥ |māyāpuruṣavijñāptyā ca dharmā
vijñātavyāḥ |yo svabhāvo parabhāvaś ca tad anujjvalitam |yad anujjvalitaṃ tan na śāmyati
