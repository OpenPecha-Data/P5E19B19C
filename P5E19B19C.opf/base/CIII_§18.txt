§18 sarvadṛṣṭigateṣu cāryaman antargato na cāntamadhyaprāptaḥaṣṭākṣaṇasamavasaraṇaś cāsi na
cāsi lakṣaṇam anuprāptaḥsaṃkleśena cāsi samo vyavadānam adhigataḥyā ca sarvasatvānām araṇā
sā bhadantasyāpi araṇāna ca tvayā dakṣiṇā viśodhyateye ca bhadantāya piṇḍapātaṃ dadati tāṃś
ca vinipātayasisarvamāraiś ca te sārdham ekahastaḥ kṛtaḥ sarvakleśāś ca te sahāyāḥyatsvabhāvāś
ca kleśās tatsvabhāvo bhadantaḥsarvasatveṣu te vadhakacittaṃ pratyupasthitamsarvabuddhāś
ca tebhyākhyātāḥ sarvabuddhadharmāṃś ca pratikrośasi na cāsi saṃghapratisaraṇaḥna ca
jātu parinirvāsyasi |evaṃ tvam imaṃ piṇḍapātaṃ pratigṛhīṇa |
